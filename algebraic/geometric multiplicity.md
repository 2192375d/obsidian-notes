# algebraic multiplicity (definition)
Let $T$ be a linear transformation
The __algebraic multiplicity__ $\operatorname{Alg}(\lambda)$ of an eigenvalue $\lambda$ is the number of times it occurs as a root of the characteristic polynomial of $T$

# geometric multiplicity (definition)
The __geometric multiplicity__ $\operatorname{Geo}(\lambda)$ of an eigenvalue $\lambda$ $\operatorname{dim}(E_{\lambda})$

## geometry $\leq$ algebra in real vector space
Let $V$ be a real vector space
if $T:V\to V$ has a real eigenvalue $\lambda$, then $\operatorname{Geo}(\lambda)\leq \operatorname{Alg}(\lambda)$
