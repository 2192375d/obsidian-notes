we can calculate CDF of transformed RVs $Z$ and/or $W$ in terms of original RVs $X,Y$ joint CDF:
$$
\begin{align}
F_{Z,W}(z,w)&=P(Z\leq z,W\leq w)\\
&=P((X,Y)\in h^{-1}((-\infty,z]\times(-\infty,w]))
\end{align}
$$
