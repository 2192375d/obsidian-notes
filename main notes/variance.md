__variance__ is measure of spread, defined from RV's mean $E(X)$
$$
V(X)=E(X^2)-\mu^2
$$
typically denoted by $\sigma^2$, where the __standard deviation__ is $\sigma$

Note that variance of a RV with only one possible value, the variance is $0$. (This means there is no spread at all).
