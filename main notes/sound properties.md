# sound properties
A __sound__ has 4 properties:
- pitch: "higher/lower"
- intensity: degree of loudness
- duration: length of time that a pitch/tone
- timbre: sound colour/character

# how sound properties are notated (on staff)
 - pitch: notes on the staff
 - intensity: dynamics, articulation markings
 - duration: rhythm notation, tempo markings
 - timbre: instrumentation, character/mood markings, articulation markings
