# conditional variance
The conditional variance of $Y$ given $X$ is 
$$
V(Y|X)=E(Y^2|X)-[E(Y|X)]^2
$$

since we consider $E(Y|X)$ as a function of $x$, we also consider $V(Y|X)=g(x)$ for some function $g(*)$
