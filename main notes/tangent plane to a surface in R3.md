## Tangent plane to a surface in $\mathbb{R}^3$
Let $L_c$ be the level surface of $f(x,y,z) = c \in \mathbb{R}^3$. 
The tangent plane of $L_c$ at the point $P(x_0, y_0, z_0)$ of $L_c$ is given by the equation
$$
\nabla f(x_{0},y_{0}.z_{0})\cdot(x-x_{0},y-y_{0}.z-z_{0})=0
$$
