# definition
A point with __cylindrical coordinates__ $(r,\theta,z)$ has Cartesian coordinates
$$
(x,y,z)=(r\cos \theta,r\sin \theta,z)
$$
(Note that cylindrical coordinates are just $\text{polar } \times \text{ cartesian}$)
