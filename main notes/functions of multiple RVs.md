# functions of multiple RVs
Consider functions of RVs $X,Y$, defining transformed RVs $Z,W$ S.T.
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
X \\
Y
\end{bmatrix}
\to
Z=h(X,Y)
\ \ \text{ or } \
\begin{bmatrix}
X \\
Y
\end{bmatrix}
\to
\begin{bmatrix}
Z \\
W
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
h_{1}(X,Y) \\
h_{2}(X,Y)
\end{bmatrix}
=\mathbf{h}(X,Y)
$$
