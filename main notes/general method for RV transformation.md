# general method
Let $X$ RV with known distribution $P(X\in B),\forall B\subset\mathbb{R}\text{ and }Y=h(X)$
Then, $$P(Y\in A)=P(X\in h^{-1}[A])$$
where $h^{-1}[A]=\{x\in\mathbb{R}:h(x)\in A\}$ is the __inverse image__ of $A$
