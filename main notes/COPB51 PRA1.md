# software developer
key responsibilities: 
- design, develop, test, and maintain software applications
- collaborate with teams to identify user needs and implement solutions
key skills:
- proficiency in Java/Python/C++/C#
- version control
- agile methodology knowledge
- strong problem-solving + debugging skills
- familar with .NET, Spring, or Django

# information technology consultant
key responsibilities
- provide technical guidance to businesses on IT systems and strategies
- analyze IT system performance and recommend improvements
key skills:
- system analysis
- communication
- technical expertise
- cybersecurity awareness
- project management

# systems designer
key responsibilities:
- create architecture for systems and networks
- ensure system functionality aligns with user and business needs
key skills:
- system architecture
- networking (IP, firewalls and network topology)
- UML & diagrams
- integration
- analytical thinking

# mobile app developer
key responsibilities:
- design and develop mobile applications for Android or IOS platforms
- optimize app performance and troubleshoot issues
key skills:
- programming (Swift (IOS), Kotlin/Java (Android), or Flutter)
- mobile frameworks
- UI/UX design
- testing
- app store deployment

# internal job search: UofT coop portal
Through the portal, you can
- View job postings for your current or future work term semesters
- Apply for jobs and schedule interviews
- Accept offers and confirm your work term
- Schedule appointments with coordinators
- Sign up for event/workshop registrations

The job board is dynamic, with new postings added/removed frequently. Most job opportunities are posted __early__ in the term.

# external job search
Make sure the company you're interested is willing to participate as coop employer.

# federal student work experience program (FSWEP)
FSWEP is a __Canadian government initiative__ that offers students paid work experience in federal departments and agencies. 
Helps in developing skills, building networks and exploring career options in the public sectore.
Available to __Canadian citizens__, __permanent residents__ and __refugees__ enrolled in full-time post-secondary studies.
Postions are in areas like __administration__, __research__, __technology__ and __communications__.

## Fintech application portals
Some organizations like __Scotiabank__ and __RBC__ have dedicated application portals where students can apply for coop positions

## red flags
Job opportunities outside of UofT coop portal can potentially have scams
Features scam messages usually contain:
- The company contacts you without an application
- Salary unusually high for the role or doesn't align with the required experience
- Questions about bank accunt details (Legimate employers will __never__ ask for banking details or payments before hiring)
- emails with spellings (e.g. hr.global.solutions@utorontoo.ca, this email has an extra o)

When seeing scam messages, report them using the __report message__ function in the inbox or forward it to __report.phishing@utoronto.ca__

## position levels
In the coop program, you are required to complete three work terms. Each "kind of" correspond to a level/role
- __entry-level roles__: help you build foundational skills
- __intermediate roles__: allow you to develop specialized skills and take on more responsibilities
- __higher-level experience/leadership opportunities__: the position you ultimately aspire to, preparing for your ideal job.
