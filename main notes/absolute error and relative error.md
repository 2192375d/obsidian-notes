# absolute error and relative error
title
$$
\begin{align}
\text{absolute error}=\text{approximate value}-\text{true value} \\
\text{relative error}=\frac{\text{absolute error}}{\text{true value}}
\end{align}
$$

Another useful way of expressing the relationship between __absolute__ and __relative__ error is
$$
\text{approximate value}=(\text{true value})(1+\text{relative error})
$$
