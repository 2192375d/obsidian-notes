Let $R=[a,b]\times[c,d]$ be a rectangle and $f:R\to \mathbb{R}$, $f(x,y)\geq 0$, then the volume of the solid that lies above (or below, if negative) the rectangle $R$ is
$$
V=\iint_{R}f(x,y)dA
$$
