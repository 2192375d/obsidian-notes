# definition
The spherical corodinates of a point with Cartesian coordinates $(x,y,z)$ are $(\rho,\theta,\phi)$ S.T.
$$
(x,y,z)=(\rho \sin \phi \cos \theta,\rho \sin \phi \sin \theta,\rho \cos \phi)
$$
, where $\rho\geq 0,0\leq \theta\leq 2\pi,0\leq \phi\leq \pi$
