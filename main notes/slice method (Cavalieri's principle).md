# Theorem
Let $S$ be a solid, then $\forall a\leq x\leq b$, let $P_{x}$ be a family of parallel planes such that
1. $S$ lies between $P_{a}$ and $P_{b}$
2. The area of the slice of $S$ cut by $P_{x}$ is $A(x)$,
Then, the volume of $S$ is
$$
\int_{a}^bA(x)dx
$$
