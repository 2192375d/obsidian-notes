# major and minor scales
- major scale: WWHWWWH (eg: C, F, B♭, E♭, G, D, A)
- natural minor scale: WHWWHWW (eg: Am, Dm, Gm, Cm, Em, Bm, F♯m)
- harmonic minor scale: WHWWH(W+H)H
- melodic minor scale: 
  ascending: WHWWWWH
  descending: WWHWWHW (do it backward)
