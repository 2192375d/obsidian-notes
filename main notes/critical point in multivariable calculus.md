# critical point
Let $f:U\to\mathbb{R}$, $x_{0}$ is a __critical point__ of $f$ if $f$ is not differentiable at $x_{0}$ or $Df(x_{0})=0$

If the point is neither a local min or a local max, then it's a __saddle point__
