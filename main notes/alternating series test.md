# theorem
Given $\sum (-1)^{n}b_{n},b_{n}>0$, if $b_{n}\geq b_{n+1},\forall n\in\mathbb{N}$ and $\lim_{ n \to \infty }b_{n}=0$, then
$$
\sum(-1)^nb_{n} \text{ converges}
$$
