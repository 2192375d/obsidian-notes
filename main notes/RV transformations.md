# RV transformations
A __transformation__ occurs when RV $X$ follows some distribution and RV $Y$ S.T.$$Y=h(X)$$
We can find distribution of $Y$ based on the distribution of $X$, using
- general method
- CDF method
- PDF method (only for continuous RVs)
