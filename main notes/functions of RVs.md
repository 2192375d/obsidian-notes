# functions of RVs
Functions of RVs are also RVs
ex: 
	$Y(*)=h(X(*))$
	$Z=h(X,Y)=X+Y$
(those are all RV's)

_example_
Consider events $A, B$, and their indicator RV's $I_A,I_B$, then
	$(1-I_A)=I_{A^C}$
	$I_A\times I_B=I_{A\cap B}$
	$I_A\times(1-I_B)=I_{A\cap B^C}$
