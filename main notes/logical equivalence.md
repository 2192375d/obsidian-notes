# logical equivalence
- double negation: $\neg \neg P$ eqv $P$
- De Morgan's law: $\neg(P\wedge Q)$ eqv $\neg P\vee \neg Q$, $\neg(P\vee Q)$ eqv $\neg P\wedge \neg Q$
- commutative law: ___too obvious___
- associative law: ___too obvious___
- identity law: $P\wedge(Q\vee \neg Q)$ eqv $P$, $P\vee(Q\wedge \neg Q)$ eqv $P$
- idempotency law: $P\wedge P$ eqv $P$, $P\vee P$ eqv $P$
- $\to$ law: $P\to Q$ eqv $\neg P\vee Q$
- $\leftrightarrow$ law: $P\leftrightarrow Q$ eqv $P\wedge Q\vee \neg P\wedge \neg Q$
